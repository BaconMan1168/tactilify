How should the active step be highlighted?

As the narration plays, the current step needs to stand out. Motion will animate whichever style you pick.

A

Sliding pill

Recommended

A lavender background pill smoothly slides down to the next step. Left edge accent bar tracks position. Feels alive — you see it move.

B

Crossfade + strikethrough

Active step glows in with a lavender background. Done steps strike through. No movement — each step fades in-place. Calmer, more document-like.
